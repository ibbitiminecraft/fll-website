What is First Lego League?

The First Lego League (FLL) is a global competition where children use LEGO robotics to solve real-world challenges. It consists of three main components:

The Robot Game: Teams design and program a robot to complete various missions.

The project Teams research a real-world problem and develop an innovative solution

The Core Values: Teams demonstrate teamwork, respect, and professionalism.
